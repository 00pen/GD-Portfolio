About Me

I am a multidisciplinary creative with a passion for turning ideas into powerful visuals. My journey started with designing event flyers for friends and small businesses, and it has grown into building brand identities, product visuals, and digital campaigns.

I am also a full-stack developer who enjoys coding interactive experiences, but this portfolio focuses on my graphic design work. I bring together storytelling, aesthetics, and strategy to create designs that connect with people. From event posters and fashion graphics to branding concepts and digital ads, each project shows my ability to adapt style, experiment with tools, and deliver polished results.

Alongside design and development, I explore music production, which strengthens my sense of rhythm, mood, and storytelling. These skills often influence how I shape visuals and creative concepts.

I may not have worked for a big company yet, but my design journey has given me the skills and confidence to grow into that space. I thrive on fast learning, exploration, and creativity, and my goal is always to create work that is not only visually appealing but also functional, purposeful, and memorable.
